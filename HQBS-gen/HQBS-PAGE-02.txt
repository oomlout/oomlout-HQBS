

<h3>HQBS - 02 - Macheng Building</h3>

<p><strong>Description</strong></p>

<p>This building is similar to Shenzhen New World Communication Electronics Market (<a href=http://oomlout.co.uk/pages/hqbs-huaqiangbeisystem-01>HQBS-01</a>). It has stalls selling an array of used (normally broken) cell phones. The general quality of the phones is higher than <a href=http://oomlout.co.uk/pages/hqbs-huaqiangbeisystem-01>HQBS-01</a> and the models are also newer.</p>
