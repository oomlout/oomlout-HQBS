

<h3>HQBS - 11 - Tai Ping Yang Security Equipments Market</h3>

<p><strong>Description</strong></p>

<p>This building specializes in anything security related. CB radios, cameras (both overt and covert), IR flood lights and other gems of surveillance. If you're looking for more security themed items there is a smaller market as well (<a href=http://oomlout.co.uk/pages/hqbs-huaqiangbeisystem-16>HQBS-16</a>(Huitong Security Products Harbour))</p>
