

<h3>HQBS - 13 - Long Sheng Accessory Market</h3>
<center><a href="http://www.flickr.com/search/?q=HQBS-B13-STREET-00-01&amp;w=33504192@N00"><img src="http://oomlout.com/oomlout-HQBS/HQBS-Photos/420/HQBS-B13-STREET-00-01.JPG" /></a></center>
<center><strong><a href="http://oomlout.co.uk/pages/hqbs-huaqiangbeisystem-main">(back to HQBS - Main)</a></strong></center>
<p><strong>Description</strong></p>

<p>This building specialises in cell phone accessories, cases, pluggies, and USB cables. While not of huge interest to those in Shenzhen for electronics, it is a fascinating building where trends can be spotted. If you have a popular cell phone, any number of monstrosities/marvels can be procured to bling it up. (Similar to (<a href=http://oomlout.co.uk/pages/hqbs-huaqiangbeisystem-09>HQBS-09</a>(Golcunda Cyber Plaza))</p>
