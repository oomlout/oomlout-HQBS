

<h3>HQBS - 15 - Wan Shang Hui</h3>
<center><a href="http://www.flickr.com/search/?q=HQBS-B15-STREET-00-01&amp;w=33504192@N00"><img src="http://oomlout.com/oomlout-HQBS/HQBS-Photos/420/HQBS-B15-STREET-00-01.JPG" /></a></center>
<center><strong><a href="http://oomlout.co.uk/pages/hqbs-huaqiangbeisystem-main">(back to HQBS - Main)</a></strong></center>
<p><strong>Description</strong></p>

<p>This building specializes in low end consumer electronics, as well as cell phone accessories, focusing on USB power back packs, SD cards and various stickers. The stickers can be both legit (ie. QC passed) and slightly suspect (ie. 32GB SanDisk SD card labels).</p>
