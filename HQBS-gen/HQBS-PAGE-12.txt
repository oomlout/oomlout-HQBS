

<h3>HQBS - 12 - Golcunda Cyber Plaza</h3>

<p><strong>Description</strong></p>

<p>This building specialises in cell phone accessories, cases, pluggies, and USB cables. While not of huge interest to those visiting Shenzhen for electronics, it is a fascinating building where trends can be spotted. If you have a popular cell phone, any number of monstrosities/marvels can be procured to bling it up. (Similar to (<a href=http://oomlout.co.uk/pages/hqbs-huaqiangbeisystem-10>HQBS-10</a>(Long Sheng Accessory Market))</p>
