<p><strong>Description</strong></p>

<p>This building is off the beaten track and looks like it may be where the LED manufacturers are starting to migrate. Everyone we dealt with here also had a small stall on the ground floor of (<a href=http://oomlout.co.uk/pages/hqbs-huaqiangbeisystem-10>HQBS-10</a>(HQ Electronics World)) and a larger office/stall here. If you're searching for lower power (ie. 5mm) LEDs or rope this is a good place to look.</p>
