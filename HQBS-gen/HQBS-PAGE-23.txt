

<h3>HQBS - 23 - Duhui Electronics City</h3>
<center><a href="http://www.flickr.com/search/?q=HQBS-B23-STREET-00-01&amp;w=33504192@N00"><img src="http://oomlout.com/oomlout-HQBS/HQBS-Photos/420/HQBS-B23-STREET-00-01.JPG" /></a></center>

<p><strong>Description</strong></p>

<p>This is one of the gems of the electronics market most notably for it's third floor. The third floor, is dominated by electronics tools. On sale are tools for manual production (soldering irons, re-balling stations etc.) but if you look hard enough, there are also brochures for companies that will sell you an entire production line. The lower floors also house an interesting collection of components stalls. For reasons we have yet to understand, this building also has the best pickings of push-buttons, with many traders selling smaller volumes and manufacturers able to take purchase orders.</p>
