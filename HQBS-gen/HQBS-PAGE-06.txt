

<h3>HQBS - 06 - SEG Market</h3>
<center><a href="http://www.flickr.com/search/?q=HQBS-B06-STREET-00-01&amp;w=33504192@N00"><img src="http://oomlout.com/oomlout-HQBS/HQBS-Photos/420/HQBS-B06-STREET-00-01.JPG" /></a></center>
<center><strong><a href="http://oomlout.co.uk/pages/hqbs-huaqiangbeisystem-main">(back to HQBS - Main)</a></strong></center>
<p><strong>Description</strong></p>

<p>This is the centre of the electronics market, a 30 storey glass tower of offices with a 9 storey base filled with stalls. Floors 01-02 are basic components. Floors 03-06 are consumer electronics, and 07-09 are factory offices with showrooms.</p>
