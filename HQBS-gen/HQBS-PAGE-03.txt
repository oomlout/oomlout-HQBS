

<h3>HQBS - 03 - Tongtiandi Telecomunication Market / Long Sheng Communtication Market</h3>
<center><a href="http://www.flickr.com/search/?q=HQBS-B03-STREET-00-01&amp;w=33504192@N00"><img src="http://oomlout.com/oomlout-HQBS/HQBS-Photos/420/HQBS-B03-STREET-00-01.JPG" /></a></center>
<center><strong><a href="http://oomlout.co.uk/pages/hqbs-huaqiangbeisystem-main">(back to HQBS - Main)</a></strong></center>
<p><strong>Description</strong></p>

<p>If you have ever wanted to purchase a replacement screen, graphics chip, PCB or socket for your cell phone, it will be available from a stall in this building (or if not in the one across the street (<a href=http://oomlout.co.uk/pages/hqbs-huaqiangbeisystem-04>HQBS-04</a>)(Pong Yuen Communication Accessories Hall)). It is a truly astonishing place. Most stalls specialise in a specific brand (or even single model) of cell phone. If you're looking for something specific, it is advisable to have the model number of the cell phone rather than the part number for the specific item. Sadly on our last visit this building wasn't open (we were too early) hence the lack of photos, we hope to rectify this on our next visit. </p>
